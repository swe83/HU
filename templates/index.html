Mission & Goal

Our mission is to increase the quality of life for unhoused people and create an active and vibrant community of both the unhoused and their allies.

Haven for Unhoused serves as:
1) a thorough ZIP code-based interactive search of places of operations for the unhoused
2) a community where people can share their ideas and resources
3) a place where people can find up-to-date information regarding homelessness
